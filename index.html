About

Clinician, Academic & Researcher

Dr. Ahmed Salman is a dual-trained Consultant Physician in Internal Medicine, Gastroenterology, and Hepatology, currently practising at Direct Endoscopy — Knox Private Hospital in Melbourne. He is a Fellow of the Royal Australasian College of Physicians (FRACP) and of the Royal Colleges of Physicians of London, Edinburgh, and Ireland, and holds a PhD in Internal Medicine from Cairo University.

His consultant career spans tertiary, regional, and private healthcare settings across three countries — including Queensland Health (Wide Bay Hospital and Princess Alexandra Hospital), private gastroenterology practice in Perth and Melbourne, WA Country Health Service, Adult Transplant Hepatology at King Faisal Specialist Hospital & Research Centre in Riyadh, and Cairo University Hospitals. He is experienced in complex inpatient and outpatient care, endoscopy service delivery, multidisciplinary practice, and clinical service development.

He serves as Associate Professor of Internal Medicine, Gastroenterology & Hepatology at the Faculty of Medicine, Cairo University (Kasr Al-Ainy), and as Senior Clinical Lecturer at the Faculty of Medicine, The University of Queensland, Australia. His clinical career spans Cairo University Hospitals — including a decade as endoscopist at the Al-Ebrashi Unit for Hepato-gastroenterology — and hospital medicine in Australia.

His practice covers diagnostic and therapeutic gastroscopy and colonoscopy, emergency gastrointestinal bleeding, chronic liver disease and cirrhosis, inflammatory bowel disease, metabolic liver disease (MASLD), and the medical care of bariatric patients, supported by advanced training in ERCP/EUS and diagnostic and interventional ultrasound.

He is certified in gastroscopy and colonoscopy by the Conjoint Committee for the Recognition of Training in Gastrointestinal Endoscopy (CCRTGE, Australia) and the New Zealand Conjoint Committee for Endoscopy, and is registered with AHPRA (Australia), the Medical Council of New Zealand, the GMC (UK), and the Egyptian Medical Syndicate as Senior Consultant of Internal Medicine & Gastroenterology.
